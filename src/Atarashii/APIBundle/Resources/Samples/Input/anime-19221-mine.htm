Synopsis

Kanade Amakusa is a high school student who is suffering from a curse called "Absolute Choices". This curse requires him to select an action from a list of two or three options that appear before him at any time, and has no control over what choices appear. Many of them are weird or perverse and as a result, the curse has tainted his reputation with his classmates and other people around him, making his life very difficult. However, one day, a choice he makes on his way home from school causes a mysterious, beautiful young girl to fall from the sky.

(Source: Wikipedia)

::WARNING:: THIS IS A REVIEW AFTER 4 EPISODES

Going into Ore no Nounai Sentakushi ga, Gakuen Love Comedy wo Zenryoku de Jama Shiteru, or simply NouCome, I didn't know what kind of comedy I was going to come to. After just the first episode I was blown away with the over-the-top, just ridiculous humor. Comedic anime such as Binbougami ga! or Kore wa Zombie Desu ka? are good examples of the type of humor that is found in NouCome.

Story (6) The story follows Kanade Amakusa, who has a unique power that he must obey or he will be severely hurt, and his everyday life dealing with his friends who are part of the "Reject 5." They are all bijin (beautiful/handsome people) but have very weird personalities so no one wants to associate with them. Kanade hates to be part of the Reject 5, because he believes that his unique power is the only reason that he is part of it. A voice, coming from inside his head, very conveniently tells Kanade to make a decision between two very bold choices. In every scenario he left with a lose-lose situation. But of course, we as the viewers, it is very humorous! And of course how can we forget our cute Chocolat who falls from the sky after the voice in his head made him decide between either Chocolat or the next door woman to fall on top on him. Her job is to help Kanade get rid of this unique power, and will also be living with him! How lucky can our protagonist get…

Art (8) Animation in NouCome is very good. Character animation pops out to me the most. Each character, and I really do mean every single character, are carefully animated. All of them have their own "charm" point which grabs your attention to them. Apart from character animation, background settings are adequately illustrated.

Sound (9) Sound is, personally, my weakest aspect of reviews. As far as I'm concerned the music in both opening and closing are appropriate for the genre that NouCome falls under.

Character (10) The characters are so funny! Character design is beautifully done, with both animation and voice/personality. Seiyuus for all four main characters are spot on according to their personalities. The interactions between Kanade and the three main heroine are just flat-out ridiculously funny.

Enjoyment (9) COMEDY COMEDY COMEDY. This is what this anime is about. School life isn't even really important in NouCome, if you saw that the teacher looks like a 5 year old girl. The scene that pops into my mind is when Yukihira was playing around with the word "boobies" with Kanade. That part had me rolling! COMEDY COMEDY COMEDY.

Overall (9) If you are looking for a straight up slap-stick comedy anime, then look no further NouCome is here!

I haven't seen a concept this far-fetched since Midori no Hibi.

Basically the premise of this story is about a guy by the name Kanade who's life revolves around Multiple choices. How this idea is contrasted is the unique part. At random times a voice in his head gives him a choice of doing two things. If he doesn't answer soon than he get's an extremely bad headache. Doesn't sound all that bad right? except for the fact that nearly all of the choices are either with him doing something vulgar, embarrassing, or something that would hurt or even kill him.

One day he is given a choice of whether he wants to have a fat lady, or a beautiful girl to fall from the sky. Fearing that his life would be in danger with the first one, he chooses the beautiful girl. This is where he meets chocolat, though she isn't given the name till later.

While trying to get the girl to tell him who she is, they learn that she can't remember anything since falling from the sky. Kanade than receives a call from god, telling him that he sent Chocolat to him to help him remove his “Absolute Truth” curse, as they so call it. And, in order to do that he must complete missions that are almost as obscure as his choices. The worst part is. If he fails even one, than he will be stuck with his curse for the rest of his life.

There....I think that sums up the basic plot of the story.

The characters that have been introduced so far all seem to be just plain weirdos. And, I mean that in the best way possible. Normal is overrated anyway. There's Yukihira with her love of verbally abusing Kanade with really bad puns that have to do with random topics. And Ouka with her spastic energy, and all around nonsensical actions. Along with Chocolat, the messenger sent from god that seems to have some form of amnesia. These three along with Kanade make quite the bit of groupies.

The Art is very good, but it's normal from what you would see from a RomCom made since 2007. Basically everyone looks like there 10, but hey you don't see me complaining. I would prefer this kind of art, than a unique kind like Special A where there legs look like they should snap in half after every step they take.

Cons: Just a few problems I have with the story.

One problem I have is when he's making some more of the embarrassing choices. He's always freaked out to do it because of everyone around. However what I don't get is why he just doesn't go somewhere else. It's not like the questions restrict his movement. Why not go to the bathroom and do it, or someplace more private. Of course some of the choices are doing things on or around things where everyone is watching, so it's only a minor problem. Doesn't really affect my opinion of the show.

Another problem with this show, is how much information I got out of 2 episodes. I mean I got the basic plot, but there is so much more than that. I don't know how there going to make this work in a 10 episode show without really rushing somethings. However it could work out well, so this is only my assumption they it will be rushed. Maybe it won't, I'll have to wait and see.

Overall 8/10. I really like it so far. The way episode 2 ended made me really eager for the next episode. If the story keeps progressing well my score might raise to a 9 but I'm going to keep it at an 8 for now.

I would recommend this anime to anyone who likes RomCom's. It is definitely something worth the watch and it won't disappoint. The comedy is really funny, and gave me a few LOL moments. The romance hasn't really started yet, but I can tell it will be really good. It's going to be another great harem folks, so stop reading this and go watch the show already. read more
